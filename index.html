Compression Modes

Lossless Cleanup

Strips duplicate metadata, Photoshop data, and redundant objects. Zero quality loss, the output is visually identical. Best for PDFs with bloated metadata from editing tools.

Compress Images

Re-encodes and downsamples embedded images with adjustable quality and DPI. Text, vectors, and fonts stay untouched. Best for PDFs with large photos or scanned content.

Flatten to Images

Renders each page as a JPEG and rebuilds the PDF. Produces the smallest files for vector-heavy documents. Text becomes rasterised and is no longer selectable.
